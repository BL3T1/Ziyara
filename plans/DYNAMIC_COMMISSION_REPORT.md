# Dynamic Commission Strategy Report: Ziyarah Platform

This report defines the management and application of company commissions within the Ziyarah Modular Monolith.

## 1. Core Pricing Rule: Hierarchical Calculation

The system follows a strict order of operations for discounts and commissions to ensure fair billing and transparent end-user pricing.

**Order of Operations**:
1.  **Provider Discount**: Applied first to the Base Price.
2.  **Company Discount**: Applied to the result of Step 1.
3.  **Company Commission**: Calculated on the final adjusted base price from Step 2.

**Master Formula**:
`Final Price = (Base_Price - Discount_Provider - Discount_Company) * (1 + (Commission_% / 100))`

*Example*: 
- Base Room Price: $100
- Provider Discount: $10 (New Base: $90)
- Company Discount: $5 (Final Base: $85)
- Commission (10%): $8.50
- **Customer Sees: $93.50**

## 2. Dynamic Commission Management

To allow for business flexibility, the commission percentage is no longer a global constant but a dynamic field manageable at the provider level.

### 2.1 Default Logic
- All new providers and existing providers (unless overridden) are subject to a **10% Default Commission**.

### 2.2 Role-Based Overrides
- **Super Admin (G1)**: Full authority to set, modify, or reset the commission percentage for any individual provider or service category.
- **Sales/General Manager (G5)**: Authority to negotiate and set custom percentages for specific high-volume providers as part of onboarding or performance reviews.

## 3. Interaction Workflow

### 3.1 Setup (Admin Dashboard)
1.  Admin navigates to the **Provider Management** module.
2.  Selects a specific provider (e.g., "Hilton Makkah").
3.  Edits the **Commission Rate** field (pre-filled with `10.00`).
4.  Saves the changes; the system updates the `provider_commission` record.

### 3.2 Application (API Layer)
1.  Customer searches for service.
2.  The Pricing Engine fetches the `base_price` from the respective module (Hotel/Taxi/etc.).
3.  The Pricing Engine fetches the `commission_rate` for that specific provider from the `sys_` or `pay_` management table.
4.  The final aggregated price is calculated and returned to the frontend.

---

## 4. Financial Accountability

- **Audit Logs**: Every change to a provider's commission rate is captured (Who changed it, Old Value, New Value, Timestamp).
- **Invoicing**: The provider sees the `Base Price` in their dashboard, while the `Total Collected` from the customer reflects the `End-User Price`. Ziyarah keeps the delta.
